How it all works

We guide you through a seamless process, ensuring a stress and hassle free experience. From initial consultation and discussion to final launch, our expert team handles every aspect of website creation with precision and care. You can trust us to bring your vision to life while keeping the entire journey smooth and effortless for you.

Discussion

Our process begins with a thorough discussion, where we pose specific questions to grasp your business model, goals, and targets. This initial phase allows us to tailor our services precisely to your needs, ensuring alignment with your objectives and vision for the project.

Project Proposal

Following a meticulous analysis of your requirements, we craft a detailed project proposal. This document outlines the identified problems, proposed solutions, estimated completion time, and final project price. It serves as a roadmap for our collaboration, providing clarity and transparency at every stage.

Project Start

Once you accept our proposal and submit half the project fee, we kickstart the project. We begin by outlining the website's basic structure and meticulously crafting all copywriting elements. Throughout the process, we prioritize your satisfaction, offering opportunities for feedback and revisions to ensure the final product exceeds your expectations.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to upgrade my existing website?

It typically takes 1 week to upgrade your existing website. However, the exact timeframe may vary depending on the complexity of the upgrades required and the size of your website.

How long does it take to build a brand new website?

To build a brand new website, we usually allocate 1-2 weeks. This timeframe may vary depending on factors such as the size and complexity of the project, as well as any specific requirements or features you'd like to include.

What are the payment terms for projects?

The payment terms for this project involve an initial upfront payment of half the total price. The remaining balance is due after you are completely satisfied with the website and before final delivery.

Do I have to provide copy-writing, images and other content?

You don't have to worry about copy-writing; we handle that, incorporating SEO-friendly content. However, if needed, we may request new photographs or videos if the existing ones don't align with the project's requirements.
