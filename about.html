Our Journey From a Dream to a Thriving Cleaning Services

Hi everyone! We're Christina & Jamie, the proud owners of Tailored Sweep Cleaning Services. Our journey began in 2023 with a simple dream: to bring sparkling clean spaces to homes and businesses. In the early days, we had no idea how we'd land our first client, but armed with determination, flyers, and a passion for cleaning, we hit the pavement.
Before long, we secured our first two customers, and their smiles and satisfaction fueled our drive. Word spread quickly about our attention to detail and friendly service, and soon, referrals started rolling in! To make it even easier for people to connect with us, we launched a Facebook business page, which became a fantastic way to reach more of our wonderful clients. What started as a small operation in the northern suburbs of Perth has now expanded city-wide, covering both northern and southern suburbs. We are beyond grateful for every client who has trusted us, and we can't wait to make your space shine!



Our Commitment More Than Just Cleaning

At Tailored Sweep Cleaning Services, we're not just cleaners—we're your partners in creating a fresh and happy environment. We clean every space as if it were our own because, for us, cleaning isn't just a job; it's a passion. Our personal touch and dedication shine through in the results, and we take pride in going the extra mile to ensure your home or business is spotless.
What sets us apart is our focus on building genuine relationships with our clients. We take the time to understand your unique cleaning needs so that every service is tailored to you. Whether it's a routine clean or a deep refresh, you can trust us to provide an exceptional cleaning experience every time. With Tailored Sweep, you're not just getting a clean space—you're getting a team that truly cares.
