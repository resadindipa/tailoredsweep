Frequently Asked Questions

How do I keep my lawn looking healthy?

Regular fertilizing every six weeks with premium fertilizer ensures a lush, green, and healthy lawn.

How often should I schedule garden cleanups?

It's best to schedule garden cleanups seasonally to manage overgrowth, remove debris, and keep plants healthy.

Why is compacting important before landscaping?

Compacting stabilizes the ground, preventing shifting and ensuring a solid foundation for structures or turf.

How frequently should driveways be pressure washed?

Driveways should be washed every 6-12 months to remove dirt, stains, and moss, preserving their appearance and safety.

Is regular garden maintenance necessary?

Yes, regular tasks like pruning, weeding, and fertilizing are essential to maintain plant health and garden aesthetics.
